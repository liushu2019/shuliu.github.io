About Me

Shu Liu is currently working as a Data Scientist at Japan Data Science Consortium Co. Ltd..
Previously, he was a Postdoctoral (PD) Research Fellow of the Japan Society for the Promotion of Science (JSPS) in Toriumi Lab at the Graduate School of Engineering, The University of Tokyo.
His research background is structural information mining in Complex Networks.
He focused on developing structure-based embedding methods for various types of networks, including signed networks, directed networks, weighted networks, and hypergraphs.
He is interested in applying these methods to practical applications, such as genomics, biomechanics, and society.
He currently serves on the steering committee for the Network Science Study Group
